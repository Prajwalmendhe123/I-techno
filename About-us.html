Who We Are

House is one of the biggest investments in everyone's life, We protect it. i- Techno Weatherproofing Solutions utilizes the most up-to-date and advance techniques in the industry for waterproofing for both new construction and remedial/under renovation projects. We look forward to helping you with all your waterproofing/weatherproofing and Masonry restoration needs. Our Team follow all projects from estimate through treatments to closeout, guaranteeing informed and knowledgeable service throughout the project. We believe in results.
